Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Decoded Lemon ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information in connection with the PapadiPark App (the "App"), which supports 21 Chunks puppet shows for schools and parents. We are committed to transparency and to safeguarding the privacy of our users and their children.

1. Information We Collect

PapadiPark requires an account to use. Depending on whether you are a school, a parent, or a member of the PapadiPark team, we may collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Account Information — your name and email address, collected when you register, and your role (school, parent, or team member).
  • Children's Information — the names of the children a parent adds to their account and the school each child is associated with. This information is provided by the parent and reviewed by the school or PapadiPark team.
  • School & Branch Details — for school accounts, the branches you manage, their addresses, and billing details.
  • Show & Booking Data — scheduled and completed shows, the performing team member, and any comments a school leaves on a show.
  • Photos & Highlights — photos and notes captured by the PapadiPark team for each show, which are then shown to the relevant schools and parents.
  • Notification Tokens — a push notification token used to deliver alerts about your shows. This is generated by the Expo push service and does not identify you personally.
  • Usage Data — basic technical information such as your device type and operating system version, used for app compatibility and stability.

We do not collect precise geolocation, and we do not process payment card details within the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve PapadiPark. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Create and manage your account and authenticate your identity.
  • Show schools and parents the upcoming and completed shows relevant to them.
  • Let parents add children and let schools or the PapadiPark team review and approve those requests.
  • Display photo highlights and notes from each show to the appropriate audience.
  • Enable schools to manage their branches, addresses, and billing details and to request new branches.
  • Send notifications about your shows where you have permitted them.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app stability.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Who Can See Your Data

Access to personal data within the App is governed by your role:

  • Parents can see their own account, the children they have added, and the shows, history, and highlights for their children's schools.
  • Schools can see their branches, the children associated with them, and the shows and highlights for those branches.
  • The PapadiPark Team (staff and administrators) can schedule shows, manage bookings, capture highlights, and approve children and branches.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. PapadiPark is built with the following protections:

  • Encrypted Connections — all data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS.
  • Authenticated Access — data is protected behind secure sign-in, and access is restricted according to your role.
  • Account Approval — new accounts and child additions are confirmed by a school or the PapadiPark team.

While we implement strong technical safeguards, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you become aware of any security concern, please contact us immediately.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter to comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time, either in-app or by contacting us (see Section 9).

6. Children's Privacy

PapadiPark is designed to be used by adults — parents, school staff, and the PapadiPark team. Information about children is added and managed by their parents or schools; children do not create or operate accounts themselves. We handle children's information only to provide the App's features and never use it for advertising. If you believe a child's information has been added without proper authority, please contact us and we will promptly investigate and remove it where appropriate.

7. Third-Party Services

PapadiPark relies on the following third-party infrastructure providers to operate the App:

  • Supabase — our backend database, authentication, and storage provider. Supabase processes and stores the data you provide on our behalf, under our instructions. Their practices are governed by the Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Expo — used to deliver push notifications about your shows. Notification delivery is handled via the Expo push service. Their practices are governed by the Expo Privacy Policy.

We do not integrate advertising networks, social media tracking pixels, or analytics SDKs that share your data with third parties.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws such as POPIA (South Africa), GDPR (EU/UK), and CCPA (California, USA) — including the right to access, correct, or delete your data and to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 9. We will respond within a reasonable time and in any case within 30 days where required by law.

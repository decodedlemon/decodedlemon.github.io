Privacy Policy

Slingshot Defender

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Decoded Lemon ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with Slingshot Defender (the "Game"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Slingshot Defender is an offline mobile game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information ("PII") such as your name, email address, phone number, location, or device identifiers.

The Game does not require account registration and does not connect to any servers operated by Decoded Lemon.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All game progress, high scores, unlocked worlds, and purchased ammo types are saved locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

3. Third-Party Services

Slingshot Defender may integrate with the following third-party services. Please review their respective privacy policies for details on how they handle data:

  • Google Play Services — used for app delivery and optional Play Games features (leaderboards/achievements if enabled). Governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob — if the Game includes advertising, AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of interest-based advertising through your device settings. Governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

We have no control over the data practices of these third parties and encourage you to review their policies directly.

4. In-App Purchases

If the Game offers in-app purchases, all transactions are processed exclusively by the Google Play Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. All billing is governed by the Google Play Terms of Service.

5. Children's Privacy

Slingshot Defender is a general-audience game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). Because we do not collect any personal data at all, there is no special handling required for children's data.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to address the situation.

6. Analytics

We do not use any analytics tools or tracking SDKs that collect behavioral data about how you play the Game.

7. Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no risk of your personal data being exposed through a breach of our systems. Local device data is protected by your device's own security mechanisms.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the Game after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data under applicable laws such as GDPR (European Union), CCPA (California), or POPIA (South Africa). As we do not collect personal data, these rights are largely not applicable. If you have any concerns, please contact us.

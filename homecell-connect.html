The Problem

Church homecell ministries rely on connection — but managing dozens of small groups across multiple zones is a logistical challenge. Attendance goes untracked, announcements get buried in group chats, and onboarding new members requires manual back-and-forth that discourages participation.

HomeCellConnect brings the entire ministry onto one platform — giving every member, leader, and zone overseer the tools they need to stay engaged and accountable.

For Members

Stay Connected with Your Community

Everything you need to stay engaged with your homecell and church, right in your pocket.

  • Service & Meeting Times — see upcoming homecell and church service schedules at a glance.
  • Announcements — read targeted messages from your homecell leader, zone, and church.
  • Attendance Tracking — mark church service attendance (including the specific service) and log homecell attendance.
  • Full History — view your complete attendance record across church services and homecell meetings.
Onboarding

Join with a Simple Scan

Getting new members into the right homecell has never been easier — no paperwork, no waiting rooms.

  • QR Code Joining — new members request to join a zone by scanning their zone leader's QR code.
  • Real-time Approval — receive instant approval or a personalised response from the zone leader.
  • Invite Codes — enter a code from your homecell leader to join a specific small group directly.
For Homecell Leaders

Manage Your Group with Confidence

Everything a homecell leader needs to shepherd their group effectively — without the admin overhead.

  • Membership Management — manage your group's membership and attendance records in one place.
  • Targeted Announcements — post messages to your homecell, zone, or the whole church.
  • Invite Codes — generate unique codes to bring new members directly into your homecell.
  • Private Notes — add pastoral notes visible only to leaders in your zone.
For Zone Leaders

Oversee Your Entire Zone

Powerful zone-wide tools to organise, approve, and empower every homecell under your care.

  • Zone Branding — set your church name, logo, and custom service times for your zone.
  • Approve Join Requests — approve or decline member requests with personalised messages.
  • Role Management — manage homecell leader roles with a full audit log of every change.
  • Districts — organise your zone into districts with appointed overseers for greater reach.

Why HomeCellConnect?

HomeCellConnect is built for real church communities — not just another generic group chat app. It respects the structure of your ministry while making connection effortless. Whether you're a first-time attendee scanning a QR code or a zone leader overseeing dozens of homecells across the city, every feature is designed to fit naturally into how ministry actually works.

With a clean, accessible design in warm gold and deep indigo, HomeCellConnect looks as beautiful as the community it serves — in light or dark mode, on any device.

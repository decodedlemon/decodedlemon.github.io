The Idea

PapadiPark brings the magic of 21 Chunks puppet shows to schools and parents in one simple app. Schools manage their bookings and branches, parents follow their children's performances, and the PapadiPark team keeps everything running smoothly behind the scenes.

One app, three audiences — each with exactly the tools they need, and nothing they don't.

Shows

Plan & Follow Every Show

See upcoming puppet shows at a glance — the date, the time, and the team member performing — so no one ever misses a moment.

  • Upcoming at a glance — every scheduled show with its date, time, and performer.
  • For schools & parents — both see the shows relevant to them, automatically.
  • Team scheduling — staff and admins schedule shows and manage bookings in-app.
Highlights

Relive Every Moment

Open any past show to enjoy the photos and notes captured by the team on the day — the smiles, the stories, and the magic.

  • Photo highlights — every completed show can carry a gallery of captured moments.
  • Notes from the team — context and stories alongside the photos.
  • Leave a comment — schools can comment on each show they've hosted.
History

Stay Up to Date

Browse this month's completed shows, keep track of what's happened, and stay connected to the team bringing the stories to life.

  • This month's shows — a clear record of every recent performance.
  • Look back any time — parents and schools revisit past shows whenever they like.
  • Always current — updates flow straight to the people who care.
Children

Approve Children in Seconds

Parents add their children — even across different schools — under one account, and schools review and approve each request quickly.

  • One account, many children — even at different schools, all in one place.
  • School-side approval — schools review and confirm the children parents request to add.
  • Add more any time — new additions are confirmed by the school or PapadiPark team.

Why PapadiPark?

Schools manage one or several branches from a single login — no profile-switching required — keeping addresses, billing details, and new branch requests in one place. Parents register once, choose their child's school, and stay in the loop on every performance.

Behind the scenes, the PapadiPark team schedules shows, manages bookings, and captures highlights. Secure sign-in, account approval, and the option to manage or remove an account at any time keep everyone's data safe.

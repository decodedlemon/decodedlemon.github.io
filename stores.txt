--- SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 80 characters) ---

Connect, grow, and belong — your homecell community in one place.


--- FULL DESCRIPTION ---

HomeCellConnect brings your church's homecell ministry to life on your phone. Whether you are a member attending your first small group meeting or a zone leader overseeing dozens of homecells across the city, HomeCellConnect gives every person the tools they need to stay connected, engaged, and accountable.

STAY CONNECTED WITH YOUR COMMUNITY
• See upcoming homecell and church service times at a glance
• Read announcements from your homecell leader, zone, and church
• View your homecell's profile and meeting schedule

TRACK YOUR ATTENDANCE
• Mark your church service attendance — including the specific service you attended
• Record your homecell attendance and log a reason when you cannot make it
• View your full attendance history across church and homecell meetings

JOIN WITH A SIMPLE SCAN
• New members can request to join a zone by scanning their zone leader's QR code
• Receive real-time approval or a personalised response from the zone leader
• Enter an invite code from your homecell leader to join directly

FOR HOMECELL LEADERS
• Manage your group's membership and attendance records
• Post announcements targeted to your homecell, zone, or church
• Generate unique invite codes for new members
• Add private notes visible to all leaders in your zone

FOR ZONE LEADERS
• Create and manage your zone, including church name, logo, and service times
• Approve or decline member join requests with personalised messages
• Share your zone QR code for easy onboarding
• Manage homecell leader roles and keep a full role change audit log
• Organise your zone into districts overseen by appointed overseers

BEAUTIFUL AND ACCESSIBLE
• Light and dark mode, or follow your device preference
• Clean, modern design with Sacred Space aesthetics — warm gold, deep indigo
• Built with accessibility in mind for all generations of users

SECURE AND PRIVATE
• All data is secured with row-level security and encrypted connections
• Roles and permissions ensure each person sees only what is relevant to them
• Disapproved or pending users have a clear, respectful path forward

HomeCellConnect is built for real church communities — not just another generic group chat app. It respects the structure of your ministry while making connection effortless.

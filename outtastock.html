The Problem

We all forget to replace things until it's too late — a smoke detector battery that dies at 2am, an HVAC filter clogged for months, engine oil overdue by thousands of miles. There's no single place to track everything that wears out or runs out in your home, car, and daily life.

OuttaStock fixes that. It tracks everything on a schedule that fits your life and reminds you before things become a problem — not after.

Dashboard

Everything at a Glance

The home dashboard groups your items into four clear sections so you always know what needs attention the moment you open the app.

  • Overdue — needs attention now, shown in red.
  • Due Soon — coming up within 7 days, shown in amber.
  • Upcoming — within the next 30 days, shown in green.
  • All Good — nothing to worry about, shown in blue.
Two Tracking Modes

Time & Usage Tracking

Track by schedule or by miles and kilometres — OuttaStock predicts your next due date automatically and updates it whenever you log a new reading.

  • Time-Based — replace every N days, weeks, or months. Works for toothbrushes, smoke detector batteries, kitchen sponges, and more.
  • Usage-Based — replace after a measured amount of use. Engine oil every 5,000 miles, tires every 50,000 km, printer ink every 500 pages.
  • Imperial & Metric — choose your measurement system; OuttaStock converts all preset intervals automatically.
Shopping List

Your Shopping List, Handled

Items that are overdue or due soon appear on your Shopping List automatically. Check them off when you've restocked, and OuttaStock resets the cycle in one tap.

  • Auto-populated — overdue and due-soon items appear without any manual input.
  • One-tap reset — check off an item to mark it replaced and restart its cycle instantly.
  • Reorder links — add a direct purchase link per item and buy what you need in seconds.
Smart Reminders

Reminders That Arrive on Time

Get notified exactly when you need to know — set multiple alerts per item, each with its own lead time. All notifications are local, private, and work without an internet connection.

  • Flexible lead times — a month before, a week before, the day before, or on the day itself.
  • Multiple alerts per item — set several reminders and customise each one independently.
  • Fully offline — local notifications only. No internet, no account, no tracking.

Why OuttaStock?

Most reminder apps are generic. OuttaStock is purpose-built for the real things in your life that wear out — from the toothbrush in your bathroom to the tyres on your car. With 40+ ready-made presets and sensible defaults, getting started takes seconds, not setup sessions.

Everything stays on your device. No account, no subscription, no tracking. OuttaStock is a tool you own, not a service you depend on.

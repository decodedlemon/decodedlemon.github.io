Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Decoded Lemon ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with Vaya (the "App"), an app that helps you discover things to do around you across South Africa. We are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

Vaya can be browsed as a guest, and you can choose to create an account to sync your saved places. Depending on how you use the App, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information — if you choose to sign in, your name and email address (or the identifier from your chosen sign-in provider) so we can sync your saves and collections across devices.
  • Saves & Collections — the experiences you favourite and the collections you create. For guests, this is stored only on your device; if you sign in, it is synced to your account.
  • Location Data — with your permission, your device's approximate or precise location is used to show experiences near you, calculate distances, and place pins on the map. You can use the App without granting location access by choosing a region manually.
  • Advertising Data — Vaya displays ads through Google AdMob, which may access an advertising identifier and limited device and usage data to serve and measure ads (see Section 5).
  • Usage & Device Data — basic technical information such as device type, operating system version, and app interactions, used for compatibility, stability, and improving the App.

We do not collect payment card details within the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve Vaya. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Show you a personalised feed of experiences relevant to where you are.
  • Power search, filters, distances, and the interactive map view.
  • Save your favourites and collections and, if you sign in, sync them across your devices.
  • Enable you to share an experience or collection with friends and family.
  • Display advertising and keep the App free to use.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve performance and content.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Location

Vaya requests access to your device's location to surface nearby experiences and provide accurate distances and map results. Granting location access is optional — if you decline, you can still browse by selecting a region manually. You can change or revoke location permission at any time in your device settings.

4. Data Stored on Your Device

If you use Vaya as a guest, your saves, collections, and preferences (such as your chosen region and theme) are stored locally on your device. Removing the App erases this locally stored data. If you sign in, this data is also associated with your account so it can be restored on other devices.

5. Third-Party Services

Vaya relies on the following third-party providers to operate the App:

  • Google AdMob — used to display advertising within the App. AdMob may collect and process an advertising identifier and limited device data to serve and measure ads. Their practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can learn about ad personalisation controls at Google Ads Settings.
  • Map & Location Services — used to display maps, place pins, and provide directions to experiences.
  • Backend & Authentication — used to store experience content and, where you sign in, to authenticate your account and sync your saves securely.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS, and account data is protected behind secure sign-in. While we implement strong technical safeguards, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you become aware of any security concern, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

We retain account data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter to comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time, either in-app or by contacting us (see Section 10).

8. Children's Privacy

Vaya is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App may display advertising and contains location-based content, so a parent or guardian should supervise use by minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws such as POPIA (South Africa), GDPR (EU/UK), and CCPA (California, USA) — including the right to access, correct, or delete your data, to object to certain processing, and to opt out of personalised advertising. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 10. We will respond within a reasonable time and in any case within 30 days where required by law.

The Problem

Academic communication between teachers, students, and parents has always been fragmented. Homework assignments get lost in group chats, students miss deadlines they weren't properly notified about, and teachers spend more time managing logistics than teaching.

Schola was built to solve this — a modern, all-in-one platform that replaces chaos with clarity.

For Teachers

Take Control of Your Classroom

Streamline your workflow and spend more time teaching, not managing paperwork.

  • Create in Seconds — assign homework to one or multiple groups simultaneously.
  • Manage Groups — organize students into dedicated groups for targeted communication.
  • Live Tracking — monitor class engagement and completion rates in real-time.
  • Share Anywhere — generate public links so parents can view homework without the app.
For Students

Never Miss a Deadline

Stay organized and ahead of your studies with a dashboard designed for focus.

  • Personalized Dashboard — see exactly what's due today and what's coming up at a glance.
  • Assignment Details — all notes, resources, and instructions in one place.
  • Progress Tracking — mark tasks complete and watch your progress grow.
  • Join with Ease — join new groups instantly using secure invite codes or share links.

Why Schola?

Schola is built for the modern educational environment. It's fast, intuitive, and removes the friction from academic communication. By providing a clear, transparent view of assignments for everyone involved, Schola helps students succeed and gives teachers back their most valuable resource — time.

Whether you're managing a single classroom or multiple study groups, Schola adapts to your workflow, not the other way around.

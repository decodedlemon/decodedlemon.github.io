The Idea

There's always something to do — you just can't always find it. Events, markets, restaurants and outdoor spots are scattered across a dozen apps, social feeds and word of mouth. Planning a weekend, a date night, or a family day out turns into endless scrolling with nothing to show for it.

Vaya brings it all together in one beautifully simple app, tuned to where you are in South Africa. Browse a photo-led feed, filter by exactly what you're in the mood for, see it on a map, save what you love, and share the plan — all in a few taps.

Discover

Discover Your Backyard

A gorgeous, photo-led feed of experiences tailored to where you are — so you always know what's worth your time, right now.

  • Happening this weekend — what's on right now, wherever you are.
  • Near you — experiences within reach, sorted by distance.
  • Hidden gems — local spots you'd never find otherwise.
Search & Filter

Filter by Your Vibe

Find exactly what you feel like doing. Search by name, then narrow it down by category, budget, distance and date — all in one flow.

  • Categories — food, outdoors, culture, wildlife, markets, family and more.
  • Budget tiers — free, R, RR or RRR to match your plans.
  • Distance & date — free and family-friendly this Saturday is three taps away.
Map View

Explore on the Map

Switch to the map to see experiences pinned around you, colour-coded by type, with quick details for each spot — perfect for finding something close by while you're out and about.

  • Location pins colour-coded by category for instant scanning.
  • Quick details on tap, with one-tap directions to get there.
  • What's nearby so you can make a whole day of it.
Save & Share

Save What You Love

Tap the heart to save the places you love and group them into collections — then share a single experience or a whole plan with friends and family in a tap.

  • Collections like "Date night ideas", "Weekend away" or "Kids' day out".
  • One-tap sharing — friends open the plan and see exactly what you mean.
  • Browse as a guest, or sign in to sync your saves across devices.

Why Vaya?

Generic listing apps treat everywhere the same. Vaya is built for South Africa — local cities and regions, prices in Rands, and experiences that celebrate everything there is to explore, from markets and museums to beaches, mountains, winelands and wildlife.

Stop scrolling endlessly and start exploring. Whether it's a weekend, a date night or a family day out, Vaya turns "there's nothing to do" into your next adventure.

Chore Equity

Bringing radical transparency and fairness to household labor through technology.

Radical Transparency

ChoreEquity is a modern, real-time mobile app designed to eliminate relationship friction by bringing radical transparency and fairness to household labor.

The "Friction Index"

Unlike traditional to-do lists that just count the number of chores completed, ChoreEquity uses a unique "Friction Index" to assign point values based on the time, effort, and difficulty of each task—acknowledging that deep-cleaning a bathroom requires more effort than hitting start on a dishwasher.

Real-Time Equity Dashboard

Built with seamless, cross-platform real-time syncing, the app features a shared "Equity Dashboard" that visually tracks the balance of labor between partners or roommates over the course of the week.

Zero Nagging Required

With a quick-log interface and zero nagging required, it helps households stay aligned, appreciated, and perfectly balanced.

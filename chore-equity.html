Your whole household, in one app

Family Equity brings everything your home runs on — chores, kids, pets, meals and the shopping list — into one shared, real-time app the whole household can see. Set up in seconds and invite your partner, housemates or family with a code or QR scan.

Fair by design

Chores earn points based on real effort — a quick tidy and a deep clean count differently — and a live equity pie shows exactly how the week's workload is splitting between everyone. A weekly leaderboard keeps it friendly. No nagging, no mental scorekeeping.

Kids & pets, sorted

Keep kids and pets in one place — daily routines, school pickups, vitamins, feeding times, health notes and important dates — all shared with everyone who needs them. Assign chores to kids and check them off on their behalf.

Plan meals, shop together

Plan the week's meals, import a recipe straight from any link, and push the ingredients to one shared shopping list that updates for everyone in real time. Beautiful light & dark themes follow your system, and your data stays private to your household.

Slingshot Defender

Drag, aim, and fire! Defend your castle from endless waves of enemies across 5 epic worlds.

Load up. Pull back. Let 'em fly.

Hordes of enemies are marching on your castle, and all that stands between them and destruction is your slingshot and a steady hand. Drag to aim, release to fire, and take down every enemy before they reach the gate.

Slingshot Defender combines intuitive touch controls with deep strategic gameplay — easy to pick up, genuinely challenging to master.

5 Worlds. 50 Stages.

Progress through five distinct environments, each with its own enemies, atmosphere, and a massive boss waiting at stage 10.

Start with basic stones and unlock increasingly devastating special ammunition as you progress. Every ammo type opens up new tactical options against different enemy types.

Enemy Variety That Keeps You Thinking

No two waves play the same. Face off against a growing roster of enemy types that force you to adapt your strategy as you progress.

  • Basic infantry
  • Fast runners
  • Armored tanks
  • Flying units
  • Underground diggers
  • Robotic soldiers

Earn, Upgrade & Chase the High Score

Collect castle bricks as you score and spend them in the shop to unlock new worlds and ammo types. Every run is a chance to push your high score further.
